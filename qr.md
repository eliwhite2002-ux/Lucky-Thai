# QR Code Targets

Generate final QR codes only after the production domain is known.

## Primary QR

```text
https://newdomain.com/order/
```

Use for direct ordering, truck signage, table cards, and social bios.

## Relaunch Flyer QR

```text
https://newdomain.com/krob-krua/
```

Use when the flyer or post is aimed at old Krob Krua customers who need the transition explained.

## Truck Signage QR

```text
https://newdomain.com/order/
```

The fastest path for people standing near the truck.

## Media QR

```text
https://newdomain.com/media-kit/
```

Use for local media, food bloggers, community calendars, or relaunch announcements.

## QR Generation Notes

- Use the final HTTPS production URL.
- Test the QR code on iPhone and Android before printing.
- Keep the QR destination on the Lucky Thai site where possible, then send customers to Square from there.
- Do not generate a permanent QR directly to Square unless the restaurant is comfortable reprinting if the Square URL changes.
